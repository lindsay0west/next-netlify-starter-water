ABOUT PAULA




Ever since I was a young girl, I had an interest in 'making things pretty.’ I would browse through magazines and catalogues, and organize my book/display shelves on a regular basis. Shortly after high school, I obtained a certificate in design, and from then on I dabbled in design. It wasn’t until moving to the states in 2016, (from Canada) that my skills ebbed into organizing. Once our kids got older, I started helping friends edit, organize and style their homes, which turned into a business I call Get Sorted!

My love of design combined with my desire to serve and help others, makes this profession ideal for me. I am passionate about organizing, and it comes naturally. When I look at a room, I can easily asses things that can be improved upon - small changes can make a BIG difference! I am a firm believer in a space that’s both aesthetically pleasing AND functional. My hope is for people to feel empowered to make changes that will enhance their lives, and give them peace of mind.

Please call or text 858-284-9623 to book your free in-home consultation. Email me at info@getsortedtoday.com with any questions or comments.
